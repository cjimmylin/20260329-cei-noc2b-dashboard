How to Read This Dashboard ▼

What this data shows: Patterns in the text of 3,044 AI governance documents from around the world.

What it cannot show: Whether governance is effective, whether it changes behavior, or whether organizations that lack formal statements are actually ungoverned.

Confidence levels: Each finding is tagged as HIGH (large sample, strong effect), MODERATE (solid evidence with known limitations), or INTERPRET WITH CAUTION (small sample or potential artifacts).

Geographic bias: This database over-represents English-language, European, and North American sources. Approximately 85% of documents are in English.

The "governance families": The six families are statistical groupings, not definitive categories. Some documents sit between families, and the grouping depends on the analysis method used.
